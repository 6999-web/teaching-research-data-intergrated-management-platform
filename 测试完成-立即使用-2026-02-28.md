## API端点

### 教研室端API
- POST `/api/auth/login` - 登录
- POST `/api/teaching-office/self-evaluation` - 创建自评表
- POST `/api/teaching-office/attachments` - 上传附件
- POST `/api/teaching-office/self-evaluation/{id}/submit` - 提交自评表

### 考评小组端API
- POST `/api/auth/login` - 登录
- GET `/api/teaching-office/attachments/{evaluation_id}` - 查询附件
- GET `/api/teaching-office/attachments/{attachment_id}/download` - 下载附件
- POST `/api/scoring/manual-score` - 提交评分
- GET `/api/scoring/all-scores/{evaluation_id}` - 查询评分

### API文档
访问：http://localhost:8000/api/docs
